Mukhyamantri Solar Agriculture Feeder Scheme

  • This scheme aims to provide dedicated solar power supply to agricultural feeders, helping farmers access reliable energy for irrigation.
  • It focuses on installing solar panels for agricultural use.

PM KUSUM Scheme

  • The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan (KUSUM) scheme supports farmers in installing solar pumps and grid-connected solar power plants.
  • It provides subsidies to reduce the initial investment burden.

Maharashtra State Solar Policy

  • This policy encourages the installation of solar power systems, offering various incentives for farmers, including financial support for solar pumps and plants to enhance energy self-sufficiency.

Suryoday Scheme

  • his initiative aims to promote solar energy in the agricultural sector by providing financial assistance for the installation of solar irrigation systems, ensuring farmers have access to sustainable energy sources.
