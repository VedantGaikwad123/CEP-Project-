Beneficiary Selection Criteria

  • Farmers with farmland having an assured source of water are eligible. However, farmers with a conventional electricity connection shall not benefit from this scheme.
  • Farmers from areas not electrified through conventional sources (i.e., by MSEDCL).
  • Farmers from remote and tribal areas.
