PM KUSUM Scheme

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha Evam Utthan Mahabhiyan

Objective: Promoting solar energy usage for agriculture.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Surakshaevam Utthaan Mahabhiyan Yojana (PM-KUSUM Scheme) was launched in March 2019 by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE), to subsidize farmers to install solar irrigation pumps for cultivation. Each farmer will receive a 60% subsidy to set up tube wells and pump sets. They will also get 30% of the total cost as a loan from the Government.

The solar pumps assist our farmers in more effective and eco-friendly irrigation. Farmers can sell extra power to the Government, enhancing their income. The scheme comprises three components:

  • Component A: Install a total of 10GW grid-connected decentralized solar plants and other renewable energy-based power plants (500KW to 2MW each).
  • Component B: Install stand-alone solar pumps of up to 7.5HP capacity.
  • Component C: Provide financial support to solarize 10 lakh grid-connected agricultural pumps.
  • The Government plans to generate 28,250 MW of power using solar plants.
  • 60% subsidy and 30% loan are provided; farmers bear only 10% of the cost.
  • Farmers can sell extra power to the Government, increasing their income.
  • Utilizing barren land for solar plants offers a stable income for landholders.
  • The solar plants are set up to allow continued cultivation.
  • Using renewable energy helps reduce farm pollution and promotes eco-friendly agriculture.

Eligible applicants include:

  • Individual farmers
  • Groups of farmers
  • Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)
  • Panchayats
  • Co-operatives
  • Water User Associations

Application steps:

  • Step 1: Visit the official portal and register.
  • Step 2: Fill out the registration form.
  • Step 3: Agree to the declaration and submit.
  • Step 4: Log in for the subsidy scheme.
  • Step 5: Complete the application form, attach documents, and submit.

Required documents:

  • Aadhar card
  • Land documents (e.g., Khasra-Khatauni)
  • Bank account passbook
  • Declaration form
  • Mobile number
  • Passport-size photo
