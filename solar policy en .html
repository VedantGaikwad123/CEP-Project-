Maharashtra State Solar Policy

Promoting Sustainable Solar Energy for Maharashtra's Future

Objectives of the Policy

  • Increase solar power generation to meet energy demands sustainably.
  • Encourage solar power usage in rural and urban areas across Maharashtra.
  • Provide subsidies and financial support for solar installations.

Key Initiatives

Solar Power in Rural Maharashtra

Urban Solar Adoption in Maharashtra

Benefits for Farmers

  • Reduced energy costs by switching to solar power for irrigation and farm activities.
  • Energy independence through solar-powered pumps and plants.
  • Additional income through surplus energy sales under the net metering scheme.
  • Contributing to a cleaner environment by reducing the dependency on fossil fuels.
